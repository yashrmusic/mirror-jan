# Omah UGC Brand Rules

## Voice

Use lowercase, soft, playful language.

Good:
- "tiny ritual"
- "soft reset"
- "daily cue"
- "begin within"
- "make your room feel better"

Avoid:
- heavy spiritual fear language
- guaranteed results
- medical claims
- fake testimonials
- pretending AI content is a real customer's review

## Claim Safety

Use:
- supports
- helps create
- a cue for
- a reminder to
- makes space for

Avoid:
- cures
- heals
- guarantees
- removes negativity permanently
- fixes anxiety
- brings wealth for sure

## Visual Style

- 9:16 vertical
- 7 to 18 seconds
- first frame should show the product or a strong before/after
- lilac, electric blue, lime, coral, black
- chunky lowercase text overlays
- phone-shot, hand-held, intimate, not overly polished

## Required Approval

Before posting, Yash approves:
- caption
- claims
- offer/price
- product availability
- DM keyword flow
