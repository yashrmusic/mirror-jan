# Omah Instagram AI + UGC Strategy

## Goal

Make Omah feel like a lifestyle ritual brand, not a heavy spiritual store.

Instagram should sell the feeling first:
- soft energy
- daily rituals
- easy self-care
- giftable objects
- calm, protection, abundance, sleep, focus

Primary outcome:
- build trust
- drive WhatsApp/catalog leads
- test winning products before buying large stock
- create a repeatable AI content engine

## Compliance Guardrails

Use safe automation only:
- AI can generate scripts, shot lists, captions, hooks, thumbnails, video edits, calendars, reply drafts, and analytics summaries.
- Publishing should use Meta Business Suite, Instagram Graph API, or approved tools that connect through Meta APIs.
- DM/comment automation should respond only to user-initiated triggers such as comments, DMs, story replies, or keywords.
- Do not use bots for mass following, mass liking, fake comments, scraping, or unsolicited cold DMs.
- Human approval is required before launching new offer flows, discount campaigns, or sensitive replies.

## Content Pillars

### 1. Ritual Looks

Short aesthetic product videos.

Formats:
- desk reset with diffuser + journal
- nightstand calm kit
- wallet/office abundance kit
- getting ready with bracelet
- candle + herbal tea wind-down

Example hooks:
- "your desk needs a softer reset"
- "3 things for calm energy before sleep"
- "this is your 30-second abundance ritual"
- "tiny objects that make your room feel better"

### 2. Founder / POV

Soft, personal brand voice without over-explaining.

Formats:
- "why Omah exists"
- "we wanted wellness without fear"
- "how to choose your first ritual"
- packing first orders
- product sourcing trips

Example hooks:
- "we are building Omah for people who want calm without the drama"
- "not everything has to be a big spiritual routine"
- "begin within, but make it easy"

### 3. Product Education

Simple, non-medical product explanations.

Formats:
- "diffuser vs candle"
- "which bracelet for which intention"
- "how to build a calm kit under Rs. 999"
- "what to gift someone who is always stressed"

Language rule:
Use "supports", "ritual", "intention", "helps you create a moment".
Avoid hard claims like "cures", "guarantees", "removes negativity permanently", "fixes anxiety".

### 4. UGC Testimonial Style

AI-generated or creator-recorded videos that feel like casual discovery.

Formats:
- unboxing
- "I did not expect this to look this cute"
- "things I keep on my desk"
- "gift ideas under Rs. 999"
- "my Sunday reset"

Important:
If using AI avatars or synthetic voices, keep them clearly brand-safe and do not present fake customer testimonials as real. Use creator-style demos, not fake reviews.

### 5. Offer + Bundle Content

Use bundles to increase AOV.

Bundles:
- Calm Kit: candle + oil + journal
- Desk Energy Kit: pyrite bracelet + diffuser + tray
- Sleep Soft Kit: tea + candle + pulse balm
- Protection Mini Kit: evil eye bracelet + car hanging + tray
- Gift Starter Kit: candle + bracelet + note card

Example hooks:
- "build a calm kit with me"
- "gift this to the friend who needs a reset"
- "under Rs. 999 ritual bundle"

## Weekly Posting System

Minimum viable cadence:
- 5 Reels per week
- 2 carousels per week
- daily Stories
- 1 Live or founder talking video every 2 weeks

Weekly schedule:
- Monday: product ritual Reel
- Tuesday: educational carousel
- Wednesday: UGC-style Reel
- Thursday: bundle / offer Reel
- Friday: founder POV or behind the scenes
- Saturday: gift guide carousel
- Sunday: reset ritual Reel

## AI Production Pipeline

### Step 1: Product Selection

Pick 3 products per week:
- one spiritual wearable
- one lifestyle item
- one bundle

Priority launch products:
- Mini Aroma Diffuser
- Essential Oil Blends
- Intention Candles
- Herbal Tea Rituals
- Energy Journal
- Evil Eye Bracelet
- Karungali 108 Mala
- Calm Kit
- Desk Energy Kit

### Step 2: AI Script Generation

For each product, generate:
- 5 hooks
- 3 short scripts
- 1 caption
- 8 hashtags
- shot list
- thumbnail text
- CTA

Script template:
1. Hook, 1-2 seconds
2. Product moment, 3-5 seconds
3. Ritual use case, 5-8 seconds
4. Benefit framing, 3-5 seconds
5. CTA, 1-2 seconds

### Step 3: Asset Creation

Best options:
- Record phone clips of real products whenever possible.
- Use AI for backgrounds, mood scenes, thumbnails, captions, and voiceovers.
- Use AI-generated UGC only for concept testing until real creator content is available.

Required clip bank:
- hand holding bracelet
- candle lighting
- diffuser mist
- tea pouring
- journal writing
- packaging orders
- tray styling
- desk before/after
- nightstand before/after

### Step 4: Editing

Edit rules:
- 9:16 vertical
- 7-18 seconds for most Reels
- bold lowercase text overlays
- fast product closeups
- keep first frame visually strong
- use brand colors: lilac, electric blue, lime, coral, black

### Step 5: Approval Queue

Before posting, AI creates a daily approval card:
- video filename
- caption
- hashtags
- CTA
- product link / WhatsApp keyword
- risk notes

Yash approves, edits, or rejects.

### Step 6: Scheduling

Use:
- Meta Business Suite for immediate no-code scheduling
- Later / Buffer / Hootsuite if you want a cleaner scheduling UI
- Instagram Graph API later if building a custom system

## Automation Flows

### Comment Keyword Flow

Example Reel CTA:
"comment CALM and we will send the starter kit"

Automation:
- User comments keyword.
- Bot replies publicly: "sent you the Omah calm kit"
- Bot sends DM with product/bundle link.
- If user replies, AI drafts helpful response.

Safe rule:
Only DM people who trigger the flow.

### Story Reply Flow

Story poll:
"what do you need today? calm / focus / abundance"

Automation:
- User taps or replies.
- Bot sends matching guide.
- If user asks price or availability, AI drafts response.

### DM Assistant Flow

DM menu:
- find my ritual
- shop under Rs. 999
- gift ideas
- track order
- talk to human

AI can handle first replies, but escalate:
- complaints
- medical questions
- refunds
- bulk orders
- payment issues

## KPIs

Track weekly:
- hook hold rate
- average watch time
- saves
- shares
- comments per Reel
- DM keyword triggers
- WhatsApp clicks
- product inquiries
- bundle inquiries
- cost per content asset

Winning content rules:
- If a Reel gets high saves, make a carousel.
- If a Reel gets comments, make a keyword DM flow.
- If a product gets DMs, make a bundle.
- If a bundle gets clicks but no orders, improve offer or price anchoring.

## Tool Stack

Start simple:
- ChatGPT/Codex: scripts, captions, strategy, analytics summaries
- CapCut: editing templates
- Canva: thumbnails and carousels
- Meta Business Suite: scheduling and inbox
- ManyChat or similar official Meta API-based tool: comment-to-DM flows
- Google Sheets/Notion: content calendar and SKU tracking

Advanced later:
- Meta Graph API for publishing
- Instagram Messaging API for compliant DMs
- n8n/Make/Zapier for approval workflows
- ElevenLabs / similar voice tools for voiceovers
- AI video tools for background scenes and b-roll

## Production Rule

Do not wait for perfect UGC.

Start with:
- 20 AI-assisted scripts
- 10 phone-shot product clips
- 5 edited Reels
- 2 carousels
- 1 keyword DM flow

Then let engagement choose the inventory.
