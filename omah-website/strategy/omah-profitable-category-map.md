# Omah Profitable Category Map

Live site reviewed: https://omah-website.vercel.app

## Current Position

Omah already has a useful umbrella:

- Wear: bracelets, malas, necklaces, small personal objects
- Place: room, desk, car, doorway, bedside objects
- Begin: guided choice by feeling: calm, protection, clarity, abundance, grounded

The best category strategy is to build around ritual ecosystems, not disconnected SKUs.

## What To Avoid Early

- Generic fashion jewellery without ritual meaning.
- Generic candles without scent story and packaging.
- Supplements before compliance, formulation, and claims review are strong.
- Low-ticket commodity SKUs that depend only on Meta ads.
- Large/heavy home decor that hurts shipping margins.

## Margin Logic

Best margin categories:
- printed cards/journals
- jewellery/charm stacks
- oils/roll-ons/mists
- candles/incense
- bundles/gift boxes

Lower margin or harder categories:
- heavy ceramics if shipped individually
- yoga mats/blocks
- regulated supplements
- commodity diffusers

## Omah Positioning To Own

Do not own "spiritual products".

Own:

"Modern Indian ritual goods for the way you live now."

Supporting lines:
- Wear it close.
- Place it where your day begins.
- Gift a softer reset.
- Begin within.

## First 30-Day Build Plan

Week 1:
- Finalize 12 SKUs across Wear, Place, Sleep, Gift.
- Source samples via Accio/Alibaba/India suppliers.
- Build Shopify product skeletons.

Week 2:
- Shoot or AI-generate product visuals.
- Add bundle pages to Omah site.
- Create Instagram launch grid and 10 reels.

Week 3:
- Launch waitlist and WhatsApp interest capture.
- Test 3 bundles with organic reels and comment-to-DM.
- Run small paid test only on best creative.

Week 4:
- Keep 5 winners, remove weak SKUs.
- Push pre-orders or first drop.
- Build refill/subscription angle for consumables.
